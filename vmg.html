Mission

To create high-quality, affordable, and delicious Pinoy polvoron using locally sourced ingredients while empowering small community members by providing sustainable livelihood opportunities and fostering a sense of pride in Filipino traditions

Vision

To be a recognized provider of premium handmade Pinoy polvoron, celebrated for promoting local heritage, supporting community growth, and inspiring others to cherish and preserve Filipino culinary culture
